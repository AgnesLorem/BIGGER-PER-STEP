## Risk Gates

- Game Pass ownership queries use per-pass generations so stale join probes cannot
  overwrite newer purchase probes.
- Destruction has one validated mutation/sync path.
- AFK overlap selection is deterministic and a locked winning zone never falls back.
- Fractional remainder is consumed only after Bigger mutation succeeds.
